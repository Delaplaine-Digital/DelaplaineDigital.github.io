About Me

I’m building Delaplaine Digital Solutions as a home for my technical projects, a portfolio of my skills, and a place to document what I’m learning.

Who I Am

I'm Eric Delaplaine, a cybersecurity graduate and hands-on builder who likes combining homelab infrastructure, network security, vehicles, and hardware projects into practical, working systems.

What I Work With

  • Homelabs & virtualization (Windows 11 Pro host, multiple VMs)
  • Firewalls & routing (OPNsense, DD-WRT, Pi-hole DNS filtering)
  • Python scripting for network and system tooling
  • Microcontrollers & embedded (ESP32, Arduino, sensors, vehicle electronics)
  • 3D printing & enclosures for electronics and vehicle projects
  • Photography and aerial footage (ground and drone-based)

What I Like Working On

  • Turning ideas into working prototypes (especially on vehicles)
  • Improving and securing home networks and lab environments
  • Designing and printing parts that actually solve a problem
  • Documenting projects with photos, write-ups, and code

What I'm Looking For

I'm building this site as a portfolio for IT, cybersecurity, and technical maker work. I'm interested in roles and projects involving infrastructure, security, hands-on IT, and practical problem solving—especially where hardware and software meet.
