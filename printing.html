Show Steer Trophy

A custom show steer trophy piece, modeled and printed as a display item. This print can be comissioned and to be printed based on the show steer.

Photos will live under /assets/printing/Photos/ once documented.

Humidifier Vent

A custom vent adapter for a humidifier setup, designed to route airflow horozontially where it’s needed. The model is designed but still needs to be printed and photographed.

Status: model ready, print and photos pending.

100 mm Coupler for Inline Air Vent

A 100 mm coupler created to interface with an inline air vent, allowing flexible ducting or additional components to be attached securely.

Designed as a functional part to solve a specific airflow and connection problem.

36 mm to 32 mm Vacuum–Sander Coupler

A reducer coupler that adapts a 36 mm vacuum hose down to a 32 mm handheld sander port. This helps with dust collection and makes mismatched tools actually work together.

A straightforward but useful adapter that shows how 3D printing can bridge odd sizes.

1990 Ford F350 Shift Knob

A custom shift knob modeled for a 1990 Ford F350. This blends functional geometry with automotive aesthetics to refresh an older interior part.
